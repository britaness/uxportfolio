- About -

I'm a Seattle-based UX Designer with experience in nonprofit and commercial projects. I built my background in visual design and a user-centered approach in my studies in the Studio Art department at Willamette University and in the UX Certificate Program at the School of Visual Concepts. I continue to push myself and build my skills as a freelance designer, and in my current role at a small nonprofit. I am interested in the role and responsibility of art, design, and technology to solve large-scale social and environmental problems and I believe in the power of data-driven design to guide innovative solutions.

When I'm not designing, you can find me painting, riding my skateboard, and trying new hot sauces. I enjoy expanding my skill set, and I've recently been teaching myself to code. This portfolio website is my first big coding challenge!
