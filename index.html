- About -

I'm a Seattle-based UX Designer with experience in nonprofit and commercial projects. I built my background in visual design and a user-centered approach in my studies in the Studio Art department at Willamette University, in the UX Certificate Program at the School of Visual Concepts, as a freelance designer, and in my current role at a small nonprofit. I am interested in the role and responsibility of art, design, and technology in solving large-scale social and environmental problems.

I enjoy thinking through complex problems and believe that the core role of UX is to help people solve problems in order to spend more time focusing on what truly matters to them.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy painting, riding my skateboard, and trying new hot sauces. I've also recently been enjoying teaching myself how to code. This portfolio website is my first big coding challenge!
