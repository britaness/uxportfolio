About

I'm a Seattle-based UX Designer with experience in nonprofit and commercial projects. I built my background in visual design and a user-centered approach in my studies in the Studio Art department at Willamette University and in the UX Certificate Program at the School of Visual Concepts. I am interested in the role and responsibility of art, design, and technology in solving large-scale social and environmental problems.

I enjoy solving complex problems and believe that the core role of UX is to help people solve problems in order to spend more time focusing on what truly matters to them.

Bonus: I've recently been enjoying teaching myself how to code. I coded this portfolio website from scratch.
